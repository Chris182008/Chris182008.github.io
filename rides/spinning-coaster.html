About this Ride

The Spinning Coaster combines the thrills of a traditional roller coaster with spinning cars that rotate freely as they travel along the track. Each car seats four riders in a face-to-face configuration, creating a unique social experience. The ride's compact footprint and family-friendly nature make it a perfect addition to our lineup of attractions.
