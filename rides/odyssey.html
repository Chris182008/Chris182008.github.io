About this Ride

Standing at an impressive 167 ft (50.9m), Odyssey is the UK's fourth tallest coaster. This thrilling Vekoma Suspended Looping Coaster features five heart-pounding inversions including a vertical loop, cobra roll, sidewinder, and corkscrew. The ride runs a single train seating 20 riders in pairs, delivering an intense experience with the second-highest G-force of any coaster in the UK.
